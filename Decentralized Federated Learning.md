Decentralized Federated Learning (DFL) goes one step further than [[Federated Learning]]. It is a fully decentralized approach to [[Machine Learning]], where no central server is necessary. Similar to FL the model is trained on end-user devices. However, instead of sending gradients to a central server for aggregation, updates are communicated over a network of peers[^1].

[^1]: [[HRS+24]]
